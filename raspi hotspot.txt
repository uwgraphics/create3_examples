Setting up hotspot from Pi:

https://ubuntu.com/core/docs/networkmanager/configure-wifi-access-points 

WLAN0 unavailable: therefore, add:
network:
  version: 2
  renderer: NetworkManager

to YAML file in /etc/netplan

Now configure network:

$ nmcli d wifi hotspot ifname wlan0 ssid RasPi_AP password r0b0t2023
